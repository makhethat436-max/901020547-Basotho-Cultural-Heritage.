CRAFTS AND ARTS

BASOTHO POTTERY

A unique mural art form characterized by intricate geometric patterns and vibrant colors, used to decoreate homes and walls. Litema symbolises Basotho identity, heritage, and connection to their land.

www.en.tierient.com

WOVEN ITEMS

These are items made from natural materials like grasses and fibres, often woven with intricate patterns and symbols that hold deep cultural significance

BEADWORK

A vibrant and meaningful part of Basotho's cultural heritage, conveying messages about identity, status and heritage through intricate designs and colors.
